How to Use the Age Calculator

Our age calculator helps you find out exactly how old you are in years, months, days, and even seconds. Simply enter your date of birth and the date you want to calculate your age for (today or any future/past date).

Features

  • Precise Calculation: Get your exact age down to the second
  • Flexible Dates: Calculate age for any date, past or future
  • Next Birthday: Find out when your next birthday is and how many days until then
  • Multiple Units: See your age in years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds

Why Calculate Your Age?

Knowing your exact age can be useful for many purposes:

  • Filling out official forms and documents
  • Planning milestone birthday celebrations
  • Calculating age for legal or insurance purposes
  • Fun trivia about how many days you've been alive
  • Planning retirement or education milestones

How Age is Calculated

Age is calculated by finding the difference between your birth date and the target date. The calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you the most accurate result possible.
